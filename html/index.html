Covid-19 Stay Aware. Protect Yourself and Your Family. Make Your Community Safe.

In these hard times, everyone is being affected very differently. Always be considerate and understanding because everyone has their own unique circumstances and hardships to bear.

Safety First Make it a Habit to Follow These Simple Rules.
Keep Your Hands Virus-Free

Always wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Protect Your Personal Space

Keep 2 meters of space from other people to avoid transmission of the virus.

Dont Trust Too Much

Think critically and always look for evidence backing news that you see or hear in all forms of media.
